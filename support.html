Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to the account settings and clicking on 'Reset Password'.

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile information, navigate to the profile settings section and make the necessary changes.

How do I manage user permissions?

Go to the 'User Management' section in the dashboard to assign or modify user roles and permissions.
